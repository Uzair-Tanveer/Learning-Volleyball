My Volleyball Experience

My name is Uzair Tanveer and I've been playing volleyball since I was 6. It was one of the first sports I started playing, and I was fortunate enough to continue playing. A week before the Men's National Team tryouts, i was playing in a game when I broke my leg pretty bad and was not able to tryout. After recovering, I continued playing and now play for fun to make sure I don't give the game up. Maybe in the future, I will go and tryout for the team and play professionally.
